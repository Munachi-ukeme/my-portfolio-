About Me

A little more about my background, where I'm headed, and what I bring to the table.

Educational Background

I am currently studying Cybersecurity at Miva Open University. My programme covers the foundations of network security, digital defence, and secure systems thinking, skills I am actively building on every day.

Alongside my degree, my background as a MERN stack developer (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) means I already understand how web applications are architected, a crucial advantage when analysing how they can be exploited or defended.

Career Aspirations

My goal is to become a Threat Hunter, a cybersecurity specialist who does not wait for alerts but actively searches through networks and systems for signs of hidden threats and attackers.

I am drawn to this role because it aligns with my lifelong mission to protect people. When tragic real-world events motivated me to seek a path in defense, my journey redirected into technology. In threat hunting, I found the ultimate digital front line. Rather than reacting to breaches after harm is done, a hunter anticipates them. Since I could not protect people physically, I chose to protect them digitally, using my development background to understand and defend systems from the inside out.
