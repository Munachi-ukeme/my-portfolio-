MoonStore

A multi-tenant SaaS platform giving African Instagram/WhatsApp vendors their own personalised online store in minutes. Built solo with the MERN stack, real-time chat (Socket.IO), and Paystack-powered payments with automated subaccount splits. Sellers manage products through a dashboard while buyers order and chat directly on the platform, no WhatsApp required. Founded and built end-to-end, from architecture to first paying customers.
